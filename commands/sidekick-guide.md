---
name: sidekick-guide
description: Interactive onboarding — an explained tour of the plugin's principle and every skill.
---

Invoke the `sidekick-guide` skill. It will:

1. Open with Sidekick's core principle — everything lives in a project, and
   the three write disciplines (free `log/`, gated `brain/`, confirmed
   `output/` and `data/`).
2. Offer a multiple-choice entry point: a full guided tour, jump to one
   skill, just the principle, or skip.
3. On the tour, walk the skills group by group, with a check-in after each
   step — clear and continue, tell me more, ask a question, or stop.
4. Answer free-text questions about any skill straight from the register.
5. End with a recap and a nudge to the next step (`/sidekick-init` on a fresh
   workspace, or starting a project).

Pure conversation — it reads only `sidekick.settings.md` for the chat
language and writes nothing. It works before `/sidekick-init` has run.
